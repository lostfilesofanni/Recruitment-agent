Job Title: AI Engineer (RAG & Agentic Systems)

Job Summary:
We are seeking an AI Engineer to join our team. You will be responsible for building next-generation RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) search systems and stateful multi-agent workflows.

Required Skills & Expertise:
- Strong software engineering experience with Python.
- Proven experience building agentic systems using LangGraph and LangChain.
- Expertise in vector databases, particularly ChromaDB, using Sentence Transformers for embeddings.
- Experience integrating OpenAI APIs and Large Language Models into workflows.
- Web search integration using tools like Tavily.
- Clean coding practices and version control with Git.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or a related technical field.
